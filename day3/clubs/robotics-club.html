Robotics Club

The Robotics Club of Kongu Engineering College promotes innovation and technical skills, encourages interest in robotics and automation, and provides a platform for students to explore and apply their knowledge through hands-on projects. We organize various activities throughout the year including robotics competitions, technical workshops, project exhibitions, and interactive sessions that help students develop problem-solving skills, teamwork, and creativity in the field of robotics.
