Free Lance Club

The Free Lance Club at Kongu Engineering College empowers students with skills to excel in the gig economy. We focus on developing freelancing capabilities across various domains including web development, graphic design, content writing, digital marketing, and more. Our mission is to prepare students for independent professional careers alongside their academic pursuits.
