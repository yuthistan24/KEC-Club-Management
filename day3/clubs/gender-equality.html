Gender Equality Club

The Gender Equality Club of Kongu Engineering College works to promote awareness, inclusivity, and equal opportunities for all genders. The club organizes various activities throughout the year, including awareness programs, workshops, discussions, and campaigns that encourage respect, empowerment, and social responsibility. It provides a supportive platform for students to express their ideas, address gender-related issues, and contribute towards building a fair and inclusive campus environment.
