Rotaract Club

The Rotaract Club of Kongu Engineering College encourages leadership, social responsibility, and community service among students. The club actively organizes social initiatives, professional development programs, and community outreach activities, providing a platform for students to develop skills, serve society, and grow as responsible individuals. Through various events conducted throughout the year—including awareness programs, service projects, and interactive sessions—the Rotaract Club fosters teamwork, ethical values, and a spirit of service.
